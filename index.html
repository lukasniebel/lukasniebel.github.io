Welcome to my web page!

I am researcher at University Münster. My research is mainly focused on the regularity theory of kinetic partial differential equations and mathematical fluid dynamics. Together with my PhD supervisor Rico Zacher, I have published several articles on the \(L^p \)-theory of linear kinetic PDEs. At the University Münster I am a member of the research group of Christian Seis where we will be studying surface tension effects in ideal fluids.


Feel free to contact me!
