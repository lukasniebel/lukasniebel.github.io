Welcome to my web page! I am a last-year PhD student at Ulm University. My research is mainly focused on the regularity theory of kinetic partial differential equations. Together with my supervisor Rico Zacher, I have published some articles on the \(L^p \)-theory of linear kinetic PDEs. Currently, we are trying to extend these results and ultimatively apply the findings to study quasilinear kinetic PDE's. I explain my research interests more detailed in the Research tab.


Feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
